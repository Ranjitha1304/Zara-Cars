    Zara cars allows up to 125 km/hr. However it is 80 km/hr in a few cities where some cars might be equipped with speed governors as per government directives. Zara cars strictly advises to follow local speed limits.
    Can I extend/ cancel/ modify?
    Yes, extensions are possible subject to availability & charges. Cancellations & modifications will attract nominal charges as per our policy.
    Booking criteria & documents?
    Min. 21 years old, have a valid original government ID (Aadhar or Passport) and an original hard copy or a DigiLocker of a driving license for “Light Motor Vehicles,” which is at least 6 months old at the time of starting the trip.
